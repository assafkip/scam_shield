## Overview
ScamShield is an offline educational game designed to teach scam awareness through interactive scenarios. We are committed to protecting your privacy and do not collect, use, store, share, or sell any personal data.

## Data Collection
**We collect no personal information.** Specifically:
- No user accounts, login credentials, or personal profiles
- No analytics, crash reporting, or usage telemetry
- No device identifiers, advertising IDs, or tracking cookies  
- No access to contacts, messages, photos, location, or other device data
- No network connections for data transmission

## Data Usage
Since we collect no data, we do not use, process, or analyze any personal information.

## Data Sharing
We do not share any data with third parties because we collect no data to share.

## Data Storage
All app content (scenarios, quizzes, progress) is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Permissions
ScamShield requests **no dangerous permissions**:
- No internet/network access required
- No access to camera, microphone, or files
- No location services
- No contact or SMS access
- The app functions entirely offline

## Children's Privacy
ScamShield is designed as general-audience educational content suitable for ages 12+. We do not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Parents can confidently allow children to use the app without privacy concerns.

## Your Rights
Since we collect no personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All app data remains on your device under your control.

## Third-Party Services
ScamShield does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Policy Changes
Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in app store listings and documentation. Given our no-data-collection approach, significant changes are unlikely.
